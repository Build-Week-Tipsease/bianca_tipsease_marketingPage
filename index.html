What is Tipsease?

Nowadays, service workers often have to wait for their employer to process their tips. With Tipsease, you can tip your favorite bartender, valet, etc directly - and they can cash out anytime they want! Tipping has never been easier or more friendly.

Features

  • Create an account as a customer or service worker
  • Link your PayPal or Venmo
  • Leave tips, ratings, and reviews
  • Service workers can cash out instantly!
